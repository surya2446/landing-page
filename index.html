Why content marketing?

As a species, we’re addicted to stories. The best stories spread like wildfire, and the best storytellers are immortalized. Here’s how telling your story right, through content marketing, can impact your business:

  • Boosts conversion
  • Expands reach & awareness
  • Educates your audience
  • Connects you to your audience,
  • Forms a community around your brand
  • Establishes trust, loyalty, and advocacy

But there’s more. When you do content marketing right, here’s what happens:

  • It brings in 6x more conversions than traditional forms of marketing
  • It costs 62% less than traditional marketing
  • It ends up generating 3x more leads than traditional marketing

That’s ROI delivered in all forms and on all fronts.

Through our content marketing services, we want you to see this ROI on your balance sheet.

Why Coding Content?

Content marketing is like a first date. If you only talk about yourself, there won’t be a second one.

At coding content, we know this very well. This is why our strategies focus on:

  • Engagement
  • Conversion
  • Ensuring it is authentic and is loved by Google and humans alike
  • Doesn’t add to the content deluge on the Internet
  • Generates measurable ROI for you

It’s a commitment. Not a campaign. Are you ready for it?
